About Us

We are Movie Buffs

24 years ago in Jalandhar,Punjab a seed of an idea was planted, a dream was shared.Inception happened.22 years on ,we look back at what we've built.Leave it up to us, and we've love to do it all over again.

  • History and Mission:

    Buzz Theatre is a platform that aims to bring the magic of theatre to audiences worldwide. Our mission is to provide a space for artists to showcase their talent and creativity, and for audiences to experience the thrill of live theatre from the comfort of their homes.
