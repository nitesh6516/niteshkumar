Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book movie tickets online?

A:To book movie tickets online, visit our website or mobile app, select your preferred movie, showtime, and seats, and proceed to payment.

Q: What payment methods are accepted for online bookings

A: We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, mobile wallets, and other online payment options.

Q: Is it necessary to create an account to book tickets online?

A: While it's not mandatory, creating an account provides a more convenient booking experience, allows you to track your bookings, and may offer exclusive deals.

Q:Do I need to print my tickets or can I show an e-ticket on my phone?

A: E-tickets on your phone are generally accepted. Ensure your phone screen is bright enough for easy scanning at the theater entrance.

Q: Can I choose my seats when booking online?

A: Yes, you can usually select your preferred seats during the booking process, subject to availability.
